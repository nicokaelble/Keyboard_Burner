How to use Keyborad Burner...

Keyboard Burner offers many cool features and options.

But what good are these options if you do not know how to use them?

That's why the following describes how to use Keyboard Burner, to get the most out of it and have fun!

1. Test your typing speed

  • To test your typing speed simply click here or on "Typing Test" in the top Navigation bar and the following screen will appear
  • Click on the green button "Start Test" and an audible and visual countdown will start
  • After the countdown, the text input field will be automatically focused and you can start typing
  • Be aware that you have to type the whole displayed quote in correctly before it will display a new quote (You surely want to train to type precisely)
